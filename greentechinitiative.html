Welcome to Greentech Initiative

Here, Waste is Wealth

We are a group of very passionate and goal driven women with focus on sustainable production and consumption as it relates to our basic needs. We believe in a green world where waste from production and consumption at every level of the value chain is carried in an environmental friendly manner The greentech initiative is an NGO focused primarily on creating awareness about the systematic/holistic approach to waste disposal in our local community. This initiative was formed to contribute its quarter to the sustainable development goal on Sustainable consumption and production.

Aims & Objectives

  1. To develop an integrated waste management system that ensures adequate collection and treatment of waste with sustainable methods.
  2. To enhance the knowledge and practices of participants at the various levels of the value chain
  3. To create awareness on waste segregation and increase recycling levels
  4. To Create wealth by identifying business opportunities available in waste recycle
