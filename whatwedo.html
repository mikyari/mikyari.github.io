Steps to waste segregation

Keep separate containers for dry and wet waste in the kitchen. Keep two bags for dry waste collection and paper and plastic, for the rest of the household waste. Keep plastic from the kitchen clean and drop into the dry waste bin. Send wet waste out of your home daily, store and send dry waste out of the home, once a week. Keep a paper bag for disposing sanitary waste.
